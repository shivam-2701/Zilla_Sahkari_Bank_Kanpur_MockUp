History of Zila Sahkari Bank Ltd. Kanpur


Zila Sahkari Bank Ltd. Kanpur, District Kanpur is registered under U.P.Co-Op. Societies Act 1965 and is Licensed by Reserve Bank of India Under B.R.Act 1949 (AACS) License No.:- R.P.C.D.L.K 30(DCCB)/2012/21.11.2012 to carry out Banking Business.The Bank commenced its Banking Business in the year 1964 and currently Bank has 29 Branches and one extenction counter in District Kanpur city and Dehat .
